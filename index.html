Experience Virtual Reality
VR Demo Day is MIT’s inaugural showcase of the latest and greatest VR technology. Over the course of the day, students will have a chance to demo VR headsets and applications and connect with industry leaders from leading VR companies.

Frequently Asked Questions
Why should I go?
If you’ve never tried VR, then this is your chance! If you have, this event will be your opportunity to demo the latest and greatest VR applications and connect with industry leaders. We'll also have food and snacks.
What should I expect?
Each student will receive a 10-15 minute in-person demo of the VR headsets and applications, and a chance to discuss the latest and greatest VR technology with industry leaders and those who know best!
What should I bring?
Just yourself! Show up between 10am to 4pm on February 7th, and the companies will set up and guide you through the VR headset technology.
What if I have more questions?
Shoot us an email at techx-specialx@mit.edu!
